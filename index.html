Spotlight: Blue Beetle Jaime Reyes

Chapter 1 of Blue Beetle: Jaime Reyes introduces Jaime, a high school student from El Paso, as he grapples with the sudden appearance of a powerful alien scarab that grants him a symbiotic relationship and powers. The scarab, a piece of technology from the alien Reach, initially manifests a full-body armor on Jaime, granting him superhuman abilities, though the process is painful at first.
